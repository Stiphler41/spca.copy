All about SPCA

SPCA Tshwane covers approximately 6200 km2 and it stretches as far as Centurion, Bronkhorstspruit, Temba, Soshanguve and Hartebeespoort. As an animal welfare organisation we acknowledge all animals as sentient beings and strive to prevent their cruelty and exploitation. We aim to achieve this by proactively promoting animal welfare, enforcing animal protection legislation, and prosecuting offenders. Our mission is to maintain a high profile within the community through visibility and interaction. We strive to do so by deploying competent personnel who project enthusiasm and commitment, supported by education and community outreach programmers.

SPCA ADOPTION PROCEDURES

HOW TO ADOPT

Are you in the market for a furry friend? Well then you arrived at the right place, and welcome! We will briefly explain how our adoption process works. You can also view our available cats and dogs here on our website (just hover over 'Adoptions' on the menu tab). Step 1 Come to our premises and visit our animals, play with them and get to know them a little. Always remember that these animals have been in their kennels for some time and may be super excited or a bit shy when you meet them at first. One of them will be sure to steal your heart! Step 2 You found your true love and just cannot wait to take them home but wait, we need your details! Once you know which animal you would like to adopt, make sure to take note of their name, go to our office and fill in the Adoption Application. Make the adoption fee payment: Our adoption fees are as follows: cats N$ 1100.00 - dogs N$ 1300.00. The adoption fees is a symbolic donation for the animal and include an animal vaccinated up to date, de-wormed, de-flea/de-ticked, spayed or neutered, and a microchip (applicable to cats and dogs, other species have other requirements). Step 3 So what happens now? Do not worry, we are almost done! Once the payment is made, we will do a house check... Find out from our office what is required to pass our house checks! Step 4 And finally you can breathe - you passed the test! We will contact you to come and pick up your new furry family member! Remember to bring a collar and a leash, and/or a cat-carrier. Congratulations on your new friend! We wish you a very happy life together and thank you for making the SPCA your first choice!
